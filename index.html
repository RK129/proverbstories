Welcome to Persian Litertature and Poetry Class!

This picture is Ferdowsi. Ferdowsi spent 32 years of his life making the epic stories we know today as Shahnameh. He lived from 940-1020.

Shahnameh is a epic tale/story made by Ferdowsi. This epic tale took 32 years and has 60,000 versuses. One of the reasons it was made was so the persian language would not be forgotten and destroyed from it's enemies. Shahnameh contains information about Persian kings, culture, litirture, history, and more.
